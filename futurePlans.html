Mission

The mission of Singapore Polytechnic is to prepare students for a dynamic and globalized. As a polytechnic for all ages, SP prepares young leaders to be life-ready, work-ready, and world-ready for the transformation of Singapore. SP focuses on students to become a young leader by providing not only academic outcomes but also critical soft skills such as problem-solving, communication, collaboration, community service, sustainability practice, and leadership opportunities. These skills sharpen students daily and open their future doors smoothly.

Additionally, SP also maintains its campus.
